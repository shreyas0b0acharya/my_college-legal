My College App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2025

My College (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you use the My College app. By using the app, you agree to the terms described below.

1. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, USN, phone number, branch, semester, academic year, lab batch, profile photo, bio, date of birth (optional), and role (student, teacher, admin).
  • Account creation date and unique user ID (UID).

b. Usage Information

  • App usage analytics (e.g., which features are accessed most often).
  • Number of questions posted, friend requests sent/received, and friends count.
  • Timestamp data for account creation and updates.

c. Notifications

  • Optional push notifications for app updates, announcements, and reminders.
  • Users may enable or disable notifications at any time in device settings.

d. Cached Data

  • Minimal local caching for faster access to profile and app data.
  • No cookies are used.

e. Advertising Data

  • Device identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID) used for serving ads via Google AdMob.
  • Information about interactions with ads (impressions, clicks, etc.).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve app features and services.
  • Manage user accounts, friend requests, and profile updates.
  • Display notes hosted via Google Drive.
  • Deliver optional notifications.
  • Analyze app usage trends to improve user experience.
  • Serve advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent unauthorized or fraudulent activity.

3. Data Storage

  • Firebase: Stores all user account information and app usage data.
  • Supabase: Stores profile pictures.
  • Google Drive: Hosts notes for download.
  • Local Device: Minimal caching of profile and app data.

4. Data Sharing

  • Google AdMob: Displays ads; may collect Advertising ID for personalized or non-personalized ads. Users can manage ad personalization in device settings.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized usage statistics.
  • No other third-party sharing occurs.

5. User Control

  • Edit profile photo, bio, and date of birth directly within the app.
  • Other account details require contacting the admin.
  • Opt in/out of notifications anytime.
  • In certain regions (EU/EEA), users may choose between personalized and non-personalized ads.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and secure authentication, to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

My College is designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Users under 18 are shown only non-personalized ads.

8. Your Rights

  • Request access, updates, or deletion of editable data.
  • For other account changes, contact the admin.
  • Withdraw consent for personalized ads.
